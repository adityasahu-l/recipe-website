Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup strong black coffee or hot water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Dry mix: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Wet ingredients: In another bowl, beat eggs, then add oil, buttermilk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Add coffee: Slowly add hot coffee to the batter and stir until the batter is smooth and thin.
  6. Pour batter: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
  7. Bake: Bake for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool: Allow cakes to cool in pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Frost: Once cooled, you can frost with chocolate frosting and decorate as desired.
  10. Serve: Slice and serve with ice cream or whipped cream.

Tips

  • Don't overmix the batter - it should be smooth but not overworked.
  • Using hot coffee enhances the chocolate flavor without making the cake taste like coffee.
  • Always bring eggs to room temperature before baking for better results.
  • Test the cake with a toothpick to ensure it's properly baked.
  • Let the cake cool completely before frosting to prevent the frosting from melting.
