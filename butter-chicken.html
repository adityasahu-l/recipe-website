Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 400ml tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Mix yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 green chili, and salt. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes (or 2 hours for better taste).
  2. Cook chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until 80% done. Keep aside.
  3. Sauté onions: In the same pan, add remaining butter and sauté onions until golden brown.
  4. Add paste: Add remaining ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add spices: Add turmeric, chili powder, and cumin powder. Stir well for 1 minute.
  6. Add tomatoes: Pour tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add cream: Add heavy cream and mix well. Let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  8. Add chicken: Add the cooked chicken pieces and garam masala. Mix gently.
  9. Simmer: Cook on low heat for 5-10 minutes until chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  10. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.

Tips

  • Marinating the chicken makes it more tender and flavorful.
  • Use boneless chicken breast for quick cooking.
  • Add more cream if you like a richer sauce.
  • You can also grill the chicken instead of pan-frying for a healthier option.
