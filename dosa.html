Dosa

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Medium
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking
  • Onions (optional)
  • Green chilies (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak: Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind: Drain and grind them separately into a smooth paste. Rice paste should be thin, urad dal paste should be thick and fluffy.
  3. Ferment: Mix both pastes together, add salt, and let it ferment for 8 hours to overnight.
  4. Heat: Heat a dosa tawa (griddle) on medium-high heat. Grease it lightly with oil.
  5. Spread: Pour a ladle of batter on the griddle and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe.
  6. Cook: Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown and crispy (about 2 minutes).
  7. Flip: Flip the dosa and cook the other side for another minute.
  8. Serve: Remove from griddle and serve hot with sambar and chutney.

Tips

  • The batter consistency is very important for perfect dosa. It should be slightly thinner than pizza dough.
  • Proper fermentation gives the dosa a unique flavor and helps make it crispy.
  • You can add onions, green chilies, or ginger-garlic paste to the batter for more flavor.
  • Use a non-stick griddle or a cast iron tawa for best results.
